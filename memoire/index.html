Welcome to PrescriptionEase

PrescriptionEase is a platform designed for doctors and pharmacists to collaborate and provide efficient healthcare services to patients. Doctors can securely log in and access patient medical history, write prescriptions, and manage patient records. Pharmacists can view prescriptions made by doctors, track patient medications, and request unavailable medications to ensure accurate dispensing of medications. Our platform aims to streamline the healthcare process, improve patient care, and enable effective communication between doctors and pharmacists.
